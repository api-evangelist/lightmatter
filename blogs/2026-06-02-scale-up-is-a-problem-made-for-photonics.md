NVIDIA's announcement at GTC Taipei 2026 that NVLink Fusion will extend to photonic interconnects marks a pivotal moment where AI infrastructure scaling meets the inevitable demands of physics. Lightmatter is contributing photonic interconnect and laser technology to enable significantly larger AI computing clusters through optical rather than electrical connections, fundamentally changing how data center scale-up architectures are designed.
